Cornelia Funke

Cornelia Funke is a german author who has written many popular books, such as The Thief Lord, Dragon Rider, the Inkheart series, and the MirrorWorld series. She was born in 1958 and graduated from the University of Hamburg with an education theory degree. She then worked as an illustrator for board games and other authors. When she decided to begin writing her own books, she started out writing childrens books. Her first major selling book, aimed at older children, was Dragon Rider. She then spent months researching for her novel Inkheart. Which was followed by two others to complete the series. Filled with stories of fantasy, adventure, and spells, her books became an instant success and sold quickly after being translated to english.
